# JobConnect Rwanda - Chatbot FAQ Database

## Category: Account & Registration

### Q: How do I create an account?
**A:** Click "Register" at the top of the page. Fill in:
- Full name (min 3 characters)
- National ID (exactly 16 digits)
- Email (must be unique)
- Role (Employee or Employer)
- Password (8+ chars with uppercase, lowercase, number, symbol)
- Confirm password

Click "Create Account" and you're done! No email verification needed.

**Related**: registration, sign up, create account, new account

---

### Q: What is a National ID?
**A:** Your Rwandan National ID is a 16-digit number found on your national identification card. You need this to register on JobConnect Rwanda. It must be exactly 16 digits with no spaces or dashes.

**Related**: national id, id number, identification

---

### Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?
**A:** No problem! On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a password reset link. Click the link in the email to create a new password.

**Related**: forgot password, reset password, can't login, password recovery

---

### Q: Can I change my email address?
**A:** Currently, email addresses cannot be changed after registration. If you need to change your email, please contact our support team at jobconnect@gmail.com.

**Related**: change email, update email, different email

### Q: Can I have both an employee and employer account?
**A:** No, each email address can only be registered once. You need to choose either Employee (if you're looking for jobs) or Employer (if you're hiring). Choose the role that best fits your primary need.

**Related**: multiple accounts, two accounts, both roles

---

### Q: Do I need to verify my email?
**A:** No! Your account is active immediately after registration. Just login and start using the platform right away.

**Related**: email verification, verify email, activate account

### Q: My email is already registered. What should I do?
**A:** This means an account already exists with that email. Try logging in instead. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password?" link. If you believe this is an error, contact support at jobconnect@gmail.com.

**Related**: email taken, email exists, already registered

### Q: What makes a strong password?
**A:** A strong password for JobConnect Rwanda must have:
- At least 8 characters
- At least 1 uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least 1 lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least 1 number (0-9)
- At least 1 special character (@, #, $, %, &, etc.)

Example: `JobSeek2025!`

## Category: Employee - Job Search & Applications

### Q: How do I search for jobs?
**A:** After logging in as an Employee:
1. Click "Jobs" in the navigation menu
2. Use the search bar to filter by:
   - Keywords (job title or description)
   - Location (Kigali, Musanze, Huye, etc.)
   - Job Type (Full Time, Part Time, Contract, Internship)
3. Click "Search"

Only active jobs with valid deadlines will appear.

**Related**: find jobs, search jobs, job search, browse jobs

## Category: Employer - Company & Jobs

### Q: How do I create a company profile?
**A:** To create your company profile:
1. Login as an Employer
2. Click "Edit Company" or go to Company Profile page
3. Fill in:
   - Company name
   - Description (about your company)
   - Website (optional)
4. Click "Save Company Profile"

You must create a company profile before posting jobs!

**Related**: company profile, create company, add company

---

### Q: How do I post a job?
**A:** To post a job:
1. Login as an Employer
2. Make sure you have a company profile
3. Click "Post Job"
4. Fill in job details:
   - Title (required)
   - Description (required, max 191 characters)
   - Requirements (optional, max 191 characters)
   - Location (optional)
   - Salary (optional)
   - Job Type (Full Time, Part Time, Contract, Internship)
   - Deadline (optional)
5. Click "Post Job"

Your job will appear immediately on the platform!

**Related**: create job, post job, add job listing

---

### Q: Why is there a character limit on job descriptions?
**A:** The system has a 191-character limit for descriptions and requirements to keep job postings concise and easy to read. Focus on the most important information. You can provide more details during the interview process.

**Related**: character limit, description limit, text limit

---

### Q: How do I edit a job posting?
**A:** To edit a job:
1. Go to your Employer Dashboard
2. Find the job in "Your Jobs" section
3. Click "Edit"
4. Update the information
5. Click "Save Changes"

Changes are visible immediately. Existing applications are not affected.

**Related**: update job, change job, modify job

---

### Q: How do I delete a job posting?
**A:** To delete a job:
1. Go to your Employer Dashboard
2. Find the job in "Your Jobs" section
3. Click "Delete"
4. Confirm deletion

⚠️ Warning: This permanently removes the job and all its applications. This cannot be undone!

**Related**: remove job, delete job, cancel job

---

### Q: When should I delete a job posting?
**A:** Delete a job when:
- The position has been filled
- The job is no longer available
- You posted it by mistake
- You want to repost with significant changes

Remember: Deletion is permanent and removes all applications!

**Related**: when to delete job, should I delete job

---

### Q: Can I set a deadline for applications?
**A:** Yes! When posting or editing a job, you can set a deadline date. After this date:
- The job will no longer appear in job searches
- Employees cannot apply
- The job remains in your dashboard for reference

If you don't set a deadline, the job stays active indefinitely.

**Related**: application deadline, job deadline, set deadline
